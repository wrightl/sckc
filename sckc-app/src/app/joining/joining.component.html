Becoming a member of Sheffield City Kayak Club is easy - just print out and return the membership form below with the correct fee to our membership secretary or hand it in to any of our club officials at any club event.

Your membership will then be promptly processed to welcome you to a family orientated club that really does put its members first - after all it's your club.

If you wish to discuss your membership further then please do not hesitate to contact our membership secretary

Pricing
