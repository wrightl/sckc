Sheffield City Kayak Club is based at the end of Kelham Island, just past the Kelham Island Museum. To get to us drive over the bridge onto Kelham island, continue past the Museum and through the arge red gates at the end of the cobbled road. See the map below for the exact location.

We have access to flat water on the River Don, as well as a small faster flowing section and a large weir - always fun for those new members

We also have ample parking, access to the toilets at the museum and a range of conveniently located pubs for a post-paddle drink!
