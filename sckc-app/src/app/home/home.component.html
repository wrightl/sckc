About

Sheffield City Kayak Club is a friendly club who aim to make kayaking accessible to everyone, young and old. We can supply all the equipment you need apart from clothing and no experience is necessary.

We have regular club sessions on the river at our home on Kelham Island on a Sunday morning and on Tuesday evening during the summer. We also have pool sessions every other Tuesday at Hillsborough Leisure Centre.

As well as our regular sessions we run regular trips around the country for beginners and more experienced paddlers on a variety on rivers, lakes and the sea in different types of boat.

To book on any of our regular sessions or trips use the links on the Booking page.
